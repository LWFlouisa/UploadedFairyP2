Voluntary-Marxism A Vunetarumaruxisima [ A Vu-neh tah-du mah-du chi-si mah ] Working class activism and advocacy information delivered by route of strictly voluntary exchanges rather than through coersion. In this case, respect is still given to self-ownership of ones body, and respect toward free speech.
